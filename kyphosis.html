Posture (Kyphosis)

Kyphosis is a spinal disorder that leads to hunched backs and stooped posture. You might have noticed that you have a bit of difficulty standing up straight nowadays, and that your back gets stiff and tired easily. This is usually due to poor posture, age-related degeneration, congenital issues, and in rare cases, underlying diseases.
