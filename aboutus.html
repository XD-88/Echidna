About the Technology

This website uses PoseNet, an AI model that is trained through machine learning to estimate the pose of a human body within a 2D image or a video frame. Pose estimation involves determining the spatial coordinates of key body parts, such as the nose, eyes, shoulders, elbows, wrists, hips, knees, and ankles.
