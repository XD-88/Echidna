The upper body, including your core and your arms, is important for better coordination, and for doing daily activities, so remember not to neglect it!

Items Needed: A soft surface (like a yoga mat or a bed), a chair and a wall

Estimated Time: 30 min

Additional Note: These exercises are slightly longer and more intense, so do go slow at first! As a starter, you can try to tackle the upper and lower body exercises on separate days. Our recommendation would be to alternate between the two every other day, and build up your strength over time if you feel up to it!

Exercise Set:

  • Supine Straight Leg Raises (10)
  • Seated Knee Lifts (10)
  • Arm Pulls (10)
  • Arm Raises (10)
  • Wall Pushups (10)
