Introduction

Hi I'm Archie, the mascot of Echidna Physiotherapy, and I was created to accompany you on this website. With my encouragement, moral support and unlimited supply of cuteness, I'm always beside you in your physiotherapy journey!

Why Echidna?

When choosing a mascot, my benevolent creators decided on an echidna because we are also known as spiny anteaters. As per my name, I am here to correct your spine through physiotherapy, allowing you to live a more comfortable, healthy and active life.

Inspiration

Another fun fact about my conceptualisation is that I was inspired by my good friend, Duo from Duolingo! I aspire to be like Duo and constantly remind you about your exercises. After all, they work best if you do them on a daily basis! So... HAVE YOU DONE YOUR EXERCISES YET?

My Name

My full name is Archie Lewis Yankovic III!

My first name, Archie, is a reminder for you to arch your back for a better posture. Another plus is that it's a super cute name!

My middle name, Lewis, was chosen as a tribute to a noble hamster who was loved by all: Lewis Hamsterton. He belonged to one of my creators, but sadly passed away recently. Let us all have a moment of silence for him.

My initials, Al Yankovic, are the same as an American musician and comedian, who was rather weird (just like my creators). AL also looks like AI, which is what we use to track your exercises!

Finally, "III" refers to the 3 person team of creators behind this website, as well as the fact that there were 3 drafts of me!
